Ugur Comlekcioglu

Summary

I am Ugur Comlekcioglu, an academic with a background in microbial ecology. My education includes a Bachelor's degree in Biology Education from Marmara University in 2000, a Master of Science degree in Biology from Kahramanmaras Sutcu Imam University in 2003, and a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Agriculture Faculty from the same university in 2009, with a focus on "Isolation of Rumen Fungi and Molecular Studies on Their Enzyme Coding Genes." I furthered my expertise through a Postdoctoral Fellowship at Ghent University, Belgium. I have held academic positions as an Associate Professor at Osmaniye Korkut Ata University and Kahramanmaras Sutcu Imam University, as well as an Assistant Professor and Research Assistant at Kahramanmaras Sutcu Imam University. I am committed to contributing to the fields of rumen microbiology through research and teaching. You can reach me at ucomlekcioglu@osmaniye.edu.tr, and I am currently working at Osmaniye Korkut Ata University, Turkey.
